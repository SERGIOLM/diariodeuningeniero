Flights

In England public transport is the best option due to the high traffic on roads and lack of parking areas in the big towns. Roads are not that good as in other countries like spain and the public automobiles are most of them old as train and bus. However the timetable for travel between little towns is high and the frecuency range is extended.

Travel to London

London has one of the underground networks more extended and biggest of the world and under it they pass through daily millions of people. Moreover is the first tube worldwide.
All the tube links are respectevely in bus. Buses in London are very popular and they are a simbol of the town. They are renewed through the time. Nevertherless most of the remain this old sign of identity and you can read on the website of the government: https://tfl.gov.uk/

Underground - tubeLondon

The tube is very useful but expensive. Weekly tickets of two areas cost around £40 and montly around £120. Single ticket is about £ 2.3 with the Oyster card. This card cost £5 and you can charge in any station. The £5 are like a deposit, so if you return the card day yo came back Spain finally you will recover the money. Taxi

Taxi in London is a medium very used to the others and it is popular also. All taxi models are similar and they follow the same old line with black color. Most of them go with some advertisements over the bodywork. They are made for travel comfortely and they can travel 5 people easily with big space for the luggage. They one of the mediums for travel more used and sometimes it is moneymaker than other travel transport, depending of the number of people onboard. After 1am tube is closed so it could be also the only medium for travel apart the bus.
