Diariodeuningeniero


Learning to design my website was one of the best ideas I had but teaching how to design and implement different modules and blocks has exceeded the personal satisfaction of learning. When I started my website, few of those who knew me doubted that my website would improve over time since when something gets into my head it is difficult to get it out, for better and for worse. But very few imagined that apart from having great followers in it, I was going to dedicate myself to teaching computer science to the next generations.

In 2019 he would enter public education in the largest institute in Guadalajara. One of the modules that I had to teach would be markup language. This module tries to establish knowledge regarding web programming, HTML and the XML language, widely used in multiple sectors.

This year I have decided to show what they can do in a module like this, a higher cycle student, whose requirements to study are to have completed high school or the middle cycle of computer science, after just 70 hours of dedication to the web and the rest is eaten by XML.
