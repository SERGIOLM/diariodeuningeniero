Learn English, courses...

If you have come to England with a poor english level or worse that you expected you should roll in a course to increase your level. Due to that you could solve daily situations in your house or in your street easily and little to little you will have a good control of all situations could ever happen.

There are thousands of english centers scattered all around the country, concentrated in the big towns, specially London. Due to the high prices between ones and anothers, the price not always says the quality of the teaching.

Livraries in London

Most of the districts in London have a livrary and they offer english class promoted by the government where it exist lot o people living in London of different nationalities. British government support the integration of the different people and first step is speak english well. It is good to avoid social esclusion and to have a create community with the neighbours. That´s not a long time ago because people note than there are a lot of people who has been living for years in england and they dont speak a good english, even people not speak english at all.
I was in the day atbarrio de Brent.
Normallly the courses were in the morning from 10:30 to 12:30 and it were taught by teachers that realise voluntary activities. Voluntary is a subject that it is very well seen in England and could help to find a good job or to roll easy in some courses or activities by the governement. To have access to the couses it was required to be registered at the livrary and once you are registered you can borrow books or access to multimedia material.
Class were amusing and people can get appreciate information through the other people who take part in the meetings. This people is very heterogeneus in terms of nationalities. Also they are events that they are announced as reading clubss or table games so the experience of learn english could be very funny and all this for free.

ESOLs courses


Los cursos y títulos ESOL son, probablemente, los más populares en España. Quizá no bajo esas siglas, pero, ¿quién no conoce el First, el Advanced o el Proficiency? Uno de sus puntos fuertes es que permiten ir aprendiendo de forma gradual, desde los más básicos YLE (orientado a jóvenes estudiantes) y KET hasta alcanzar el nivel C2 del Marco de Referencia Europea con el CPE. Además, una vez que superas las pruebas de cada nivel, la certificación es válida para siempre.

WAES / Westminster Education Adult Service

I hugely recommend roll in public centers supported by the government like WAES and also complement this studies with other centers or academies.

They are centers suchs as the Westminster Education Adult Service where they give formation abouth different areas like language and in this case english.
This courses give accreditation that they go in parallel to Cambridge and whose exams they are more focus to daily matters insted of accademic or bussiness matters. They are call ESOLs courses.
The prices are free and if you fulfill the requirements because you don´t earn so much money you can go for free. Advance level is 120 pound all course, about 120 hours. It exist the possibility of do compatible with your job becase there is a course intesive on saturdays of 6 hours that starts at 10 am, that was the course I did.

Callan Method


Callan Method is a system derived of learning natural method: it prioritize conversation, auditive comprehension and memory through a practise focus point of view. The goal is the pupil achieve a good self confidence to speak english at first moment, such as they teach in the class I was enrolled.


El objetivo es que el alumno se suelte a hablar inglés desde el primer momento, tal y como se enseña en las academias regidas por este método.


There are a lot of schools with this method in London and the prices vary depending of they were. They are between 100 pounds and 300 pounds monthly by 2 hours and 5 days per week. It is depending also of the timetable it is more asked for like is morning timetable.
