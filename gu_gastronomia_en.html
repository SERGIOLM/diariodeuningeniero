The region of Guadalajara is diverse in terrain and nature so we have different products, all of great quality. The diet is based on a cluster of products based on agriculture, where most is based on planting grain, on livestock and fisheries.

Would highlight several areas in the province where a product stand out more than others.




The most typical dishes of the region are as follows:

  • Crumb. (1)
  • Gruel.
  • Beans. (5)
  • Garlic soup.
  • Lamb and goat. (2, 3)
  • Trout. (4)
  • Mushrooms and chanterelles.(6)
  • Products big game such as wild boar and deer.
  • Small game, quail, partridge or rabbit.


Desserts and sweets with more fame:

  • Baba.(1)
  • "Legged cow".(2)
  • Crispines. (3)
  • Shortbread. (4)
  • Sweet honey.
  • Tortas.
