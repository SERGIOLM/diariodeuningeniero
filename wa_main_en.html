The purpose of this site is to help those who plan to move to another country by presenting basic advice and showing simple ways to achieve objectives that will make us start in an easier way. This last part is what will differentiate this site from any other. Before embarking on an adventure to an outside country, it is a good idea to inquire about the country where we want to work. For this, from my point of view, we will have to study the following points:

  • Offer of the demanded position.
  • Required language.
  • Expenses: Housing, transportation and food.
  • Economy in the country: Currency, interest rates.
  • Ease to find foods that are familiar to us.
  • Leisure, sport, culture.

Once the destination country has been chosen, it will be necessary to select the city where the search will begin based on the fact that a contract has not been made before traveling. My recommendation is to look for a big city, to increase the chances of being called and to integrate and get friendships more easily.

Also, if we do not know how to cook or are familiar with making the purchase, we should inform ourselves about the local foods that are available. In the case that there are not many products or that are too expensive we must assume these disadvantages. It is also true that in Europe vegetables and fruits are almost all imported from the south and due to transport and the addition of more preservatives and the time of arrival will not be the same as eating these products in our country.

Another quite important point is the change of schedule that we must assume to do various activities since the sun usually sets in the north. In addition we will have scarce hours of light that we will have to take advantage of since the lack of light can cause us many problems and in Spain we are used to having sun almost every day. Although the mind is strong everything affects and could affect us much more any situation. The sun stimulates the hormone of melatonin that influences sleep and in many aspects so it should be accompanied by supplements.
